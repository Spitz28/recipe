Crispy Baked Chicken Thighs

Crispy Baked
Chicken Thighs

Prep time - 5 minutes Total time - ~40 minutes Servings - 8
Cook time - ~35 minutes
Calories - 410kcal

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds chicken thighs (About 6-8)

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

  • 2 teaspoons salt

  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder

  • 2 teaspoons onion powder (optional)

  • 2 teaspoons italian seasoning

  • 1 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.

  2. Line a large baking sheet with foil for a fast and easy cleanup.

  3. Remove chicken thighs from the packaging and pat them dry with paper towels. Arrange the chicken
    on the baking sheet and dribble them in the olive oil.

  4. Rub the olive oil into the chicken

  5. Mix the salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder (optional), and italian seasoning together in a bowl.

  6. Sprinkle the spices over the chicken evenly.

  7. Bake the chicken in the preheated oven for 35-45 minutes.*

Notes

*Chicken should reach an internal temprature of 165 degrees before consuming
